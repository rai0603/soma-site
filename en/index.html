SEC.04 / SIGNATURE
DUO TALK_

Two AIs, talking on your desktop

Two characters appear together, trade lines on their own, and can pull you in anytime. Like a private AI podcast made just for you — watch the show, or jump in.

Hey, today's topic is "the ultimate work playlist," right? I'll kick things off~
Hmph, you always go first... Fine — then I'll be the one roasting your picks.

The two of them chat about any topic you set while you watch and listen — like your own AI podcast.

>>
Smooth, no dead air
The next line is pre-generated while one is speaking — near-zero gaps between turns.
>>
Voices never collide
One finishes before the other begins — strictly sequential speech.
>>
Full control
Adjust position / size / angle, and pause, resume, or change the pace mid-session; the toolbar auto-fades once they start talking.
>>
Mix and match ・ costs stay capped
Pair one Live2D with one VRM in the same scene; a built-in turn limit keeps costs in check.
